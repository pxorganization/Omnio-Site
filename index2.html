Make energy usage Smart | Cost-efficient | User-friendly

Omnio helps businesses cut electricity costs, balance consumption, and meet sustainability goals. By automated, real-time appliance scheduling based on dynamic pricing

"Energy prices are unpredictable, Business costs shouldn’t be"

How It Works

Automate Scheduling

Omnio creates a cost-efficient weekly schedule tailored to your business-specific preferences and availability, maximizing savings by using electricity when prices are lowest

Adapt in Real Time

Omnio dynamically adjusts your schedule as energy prices and conditions change, ensuring continuous savings and optimal load distribution

Deploy Effortlessly

No technical skills needed—simply define device rules, and Omnio manages everything, making energy optimization effortless

Get Actionable Insights

View detailed insights on energy consumption, cost avoidance, and sustainability impact, empowering smarter business decisions

Why It Matters
