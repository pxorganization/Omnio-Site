Omnio

We help households and businesses reduce electricity costs and gain full control by automatically scheduling appliances when energy is cheapest.

"Smart appliance scheduling that cuts your energy costs without changing your routine."

How It Works

Optimize

Omnio crafts a cost-efficient weekly schedule tailored to your preferences and device availability, maximizing savings by using electricity when prices are lowest.

Adapt in Real Time

Omnio dynamically adjusts your schedule as energy prices and conditions change, ensuring continuous savings and optimal load distribution.

Simple & Accessible

No technical skills needed—input your device preferences, and Omnio manages everything, making energy optimization effortless.

Report

Omnio provides clear insights into energy usage, cost savings, and sustainability impact through intuitive reports, empowering smarter decisions.

Why It Matters

Save Money

Electricity prices fluctuate. We schedule appliances to run when costs are lowest, saving you money effortlessly.

Stay in Control

Set your preferences and monitor everything with real-time dashboards that keep you informed and in charge.

View Your Costs

Track energy usage and savings with hourly, euro-level transparency for complete clarity.
