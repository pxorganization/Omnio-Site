FAQ

Our platform leverages dynamic electricity pricing to identify the lowest-cost time windows within your provider’s plan. Based on your consumption profile and operational needs, we schedule your device usage during these low-price periods, significantly reducing your overall energy costs.

Our solution is ideal for businesses with high or time-sensitive energy consumption, such as hotels and EV fleets, also can perfect fit for b2b2c sectors like Long-Term rental companies and energy providers. We tailor the platform to each company’s specific needs and constraints to ensure maximum benefit.

You simply need first to talk with us, connect your energy consumption habits and your provider’s pricing plan. IoT hardware is not required but the more data and sensors you can provide, the more accurate and valuable our recommendations will be.

We support both fixed tariffs (e.g. day/night plans) and dynamic pricing models like hourly. The more dynamic the pricing model, the greater the savings potential with our system.

This is one of the features we’re actively developing. Very soon, you’ll be able to compare available energy plans and see which provider or pricing scheme offers the best deal based on your actual consumption patterns.

All information is securely stored following European data protection regulations. Any analysis we provide to third parties is based on anonymized, aggregated insights — and we never share personal or sensitive data without your explicit consent.
